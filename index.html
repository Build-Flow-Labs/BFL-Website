// CORE_PRODUCT

BuildGuard

Continuous compliance for GitHub. BuildGuard scans your entire org, evaluates 10 policies mapped to 6 compliance frameworks, records evidence in PostgreSQL, and auto-remediates violations via pull requests.

01 10 Compliance Policies (Security, Governance, SOX)
02 6 Framework Mappings — SOC2, SOX, NIST, ISO, PCI, FedRAMP
03 Auto-Remediation via Pull Requests
04 PostgreSQL Evidence Ledger & Audit Trail
33 Controls Framework Coverage
OPA / Rego Extensible Engine
